Our Mission

Founded in 2025, Right to Thrive is a non-profit organization focused on empowering the community. Through the help of legal professionals, we are able to host many projects within the community to make our society a more fair and just place. Our work with The Sidewalk Stories Project helps even further to encourage empathy, and give everyone an equal opportunity to thrive.

Legal Clinics

  • Free 1-on-1 consultations with volunteer lawyers or law students
  • Help with forms (e.g., expungement, immigration, name change)

Workshops & Seminars

  • "Know Your Rights" sessions (housing, employment, immigration, policing)
  • Tenant and Landlord Rights
  • Small Claims Court 101
  • Legal Basics for Entrepreneurs

Youth Programs

  • Law-themed summer camps for teens
  • Law students and lawyers teach formulation of an argument, public speech, mock-trial runs
  • Community engagement: who the law protects and why it matters
